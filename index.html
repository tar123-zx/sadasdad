About Me

I’ve always had a deep love for sports. I enjoy playing and watching almost every sport, but the ones that truly excite me are badminton and football. Badminton challenges my reflexes and sharpens my focus, while football brings out the thrill of strategy, teamwork, and fast-paced action. I try to stay active by playing both whenever I get the chance, whether it's a casual game with friends or participating in local events. For me, sports are not just a hobby—they’re a way to stay energized, healthy, and connected with others. They also help me balance my academic life by giving me an outlet to refresh and stay motivated
